Digestive Issues

Three to Four Years of Fatigue, Bloating, and Diarrhea, Resolved by One Formula

Recovered in 4 weeks Digestive Issues

Three to four years of fatigue, a belly that stayed constantly bloated, and diarrhea that struck without much warning, worst after lunch. This man in his early thirties had a decent enough build but noticeably low energy, and symptom by symptom, the upper and lower halves of his body seemed to be telling two different stories.

His mouth was frequently dry, with a sweetish odor and sometimes a bitter taste. His chest was often tight and stiff — signs of heat accumulating above. But his stools were always loose, and cold food, dairy, spicy food, or anything oily sent him straight into diarrhea, with poor appetite and low stamina — a clear signal of the opposite, a digestive system running cold and weak below.

When the Top and Bottom of the Body Tell Different Stories

Heat pooling and causing tightness and thirst above, cold weakening digestion and causing loose stools below — on the surface this looks like a contradiction, but it's a combination that shows up together in the same body fairly often, especially alongside an intolerance to both heat and cold.

Bringing Two Opposing Signals Into One Formula

A single formula was chosen that addressed both at once: clearing the heat sitting above, while warming and strengthening digestion below, given as a four-week course.

Resolving From the Bottom Up, in Sequence

The order in which things improved was striking. The bowel movements responded first, the loose, frequent stools returning to normal. Next, the bloating began to ease. Last of all, the fatigue he'd been carrying for years lifted noticeably. It was almost as though the formula stabilized the cold pattern at the deepest layer of his digestion first, then worked its way upward, resolving the stagnation built up on top of it. By the end of the four-week course, all three symptoms had improved markedly and treatment concluded.
